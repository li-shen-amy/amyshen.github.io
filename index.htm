ABOUT

Li Shen is currently a Postdoctoral Scholar and Research Associate at Zilkha Neurogenetic Institute in the University of Southern California. With Biomedical Engineering Ph.D., she is an experienced Neuroengineer and Data Scientist with 10+ years of research in developing and utilizing innovative engineering technique and data analysis approach in multiple fields, especially in decipering brain circuits underlying sensory and emotions, publishing 8 research papers with more than 190 citations to date. She is skilled in data analysis and programming.

Research Fields

  • Innovative approach to readout and manipulate single neuron activity
  • Sensory and emotion related neural circuits
  • Network and neural-based computational modeling
  • Innovative engineering or data approaches for automatic experimental control
