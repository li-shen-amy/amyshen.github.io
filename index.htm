ABOUT

Li Shen is currently a Postdoctoral Scholar and Research Associate at Zilkha Neurogenetic Institute in the University of Southern California. With Biomedical Engineering Ph.D., she is an experienced Neuroscientist and Neuroengineer with 10 years of research in developing and utilizing innovative techniques to deciper brain circuits underlying sensory and emotions, bringing out 8 publications with more than 158 citations. She is skilled in data analysis and programming.

Research Fields

  • Auditory neuroscience.
  • Fear and reward learning related neural circuit.
  • Acoutstic and neuronal signal processing
  • Network and neural-based computational modeling
